Global Finalist of NASA Space Apps Challenge

NASA Space Apps Challenge Bangladesh 2018

It's a competition of NASA's Space Apps Challenge. Our proposed device will have necessary equipment to carry out several lab tests which are crucial to detect bio-signatures, weather, soil condition, water detection in Mars. Those data will help them to sustain their life in Mars
